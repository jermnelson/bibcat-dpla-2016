Subjects

Concepts that may be subjects include topics, places, temporal expressions, events, works, instances, items, agents, etc.
Overview of the BIBFRAME 2.0 Model
BIBFRAME Person

A BIBFRAME Person can serve as a bibliographic "subject" for a BIBFRAME Work or Instance.

<http://bibcat.org/f2f0aa80-2c55-11e6-811c-a8667f19014b> a bf:Work;
    bf:subject [ a bf:Person ;
        owl:sameAs <http://id.loc.gov/authorities/names/n78095603> ;
        rdfs:label "Francis, of Assisi, Saint, 1182-1226"@en
        ] .
BIBFRAME Organizations

A BIBFRAME Organization can serve as a bibliographic "subject" for a BIBFRAME Work or Instance.

<http://bibcat.org/a02e8a76-2e78-11e6-97dd-005056c00008> a bf:Text ;
    bf:identifiedBy [ a bf:Isbn ;
            rdf:value "0837195349" ] ;
    bf:subject <http://id.loc.gov/authorities/names/n87858369> ;
    bf:title [ a bf:WorkTitle ;
            rdfs:label "Buddhism in America : the social organization of an ethnic religious institution"@en ] ;
    relator:aut <http://id.loc.gov/authorities/names/no99078102> .

<http://id.loc.gov/authorities/names/n87858369> rdfs:label "Buddhist Churches of America"@en .
BIBFRAME Topics

Concept or area of knowledge, the BIBFRAME Topic class can be used for Library of Congress Subject Headings and server as the bibliographic "subject" to BIBFRAME Works, Instances, or Items.

<http://bibcat.org/0f625458-2e7f-11e6-b676-005056c00008> a bf:Electronic ;
    rdfs:label "What is Islam? : the importance of being Islamic / by Shahab Ahmed"@en ;
    bf:identifiedBy [ a bf:Isbn ;
            rdf:value "9781400873586 (electronic bk.)" ] ;
    bf:subject [ a bf:Topic ;
            rdfs:label "Islam"@en ;
            schema:url <http://id.loc.gov/authorities/subjects/sh85068390> ] ;
    bf:title [ a bf:InstanceTitle ;
            bf:mainTitle "What is Islam?"@en ;
            bf:subtitle "the importance of being Islamic"@en ] .
