BIBFRAME Agents

Agents are people, organizations, jurisdictions, etc., associated with a Work or Instance through roles such as author, editor, artist, photographer, composer, illustrator, etc.
Overview of the BIBFRAME 2.0 Model

Person is an Agent subclass

St. Francis of Assisi

As a person, subject, or creator, in a BIBFRAME linked catalog, the person St. Francis of Assisi resolves to a single URI, in this the Library of Congress Name Authority URI.

source

<http://id.loc.gov/authorities/names/n78095603> a bf:Person ;
    rdfs:label "Saint Francis of Assisi" ;
    owl:sameAs <http://dbpedia.org/c/8CO6RG> ;
    schema:birthData 1182 ;
    schema:deathDate 1226 .
